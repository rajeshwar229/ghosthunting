    Help Modal

    The goal of the game is to guess the opponents number, before they guess yours. At first, you need to select your number(which should be in between the range) and the range of numbers to play. Once the game starts, you and the system will keep guessing each others numbers in turns. You have to wait for the system till it guesses. Avoid click on your number, or you will loose. You can see your number on top. Whoever guesses correctly first wins the game. If both of you chosen same number, no one wins. Good luck for you and enjoy the game!
